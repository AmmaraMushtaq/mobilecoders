What are the technologies used for developing a Blockchain application?
The technologies used for developing a Blockchain application are stellar, Multichain, Hyperledger, Ethereum, EOS, and NEM.
How much does it cost to build a blockchain app?
Estimating the cost of Blockchain completely depends on multiple factors, such as the application complexity, the number of stakeholders engaging with the app, industry practices, etc.
Why should businesses invest in Blockchain development?
Here listed some of the reasons why businesses should go for blockchain development:
  • Complete Automation
  • No duplication of data
  • Greater transparency
  • Fast and smooth transactions
  • High data security
