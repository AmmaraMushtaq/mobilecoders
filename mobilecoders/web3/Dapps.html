What is a decentralized application?
A decentralized application is an application built on a decentralized network that can operate through smart contract utilization and runs on a blockchain or distributed ledger system.
What are the different advantages of dapp?
Here is a list of some advantages that businesses can get from Dapp development:
  • Ensure transparency
  • High-level data safety
  • Block immunity
  • Lack of censorship
  • Development flexibility
How much does an Dapp cost?
The decentralized application will cost you between 10,000- $100,000 to build a full-fledged Dapp. The cost also depends on the location across the globe.
