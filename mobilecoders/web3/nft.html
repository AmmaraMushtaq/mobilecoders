FAQs

Got specific questions? Get clear answers

What do you mean by NFTs?
NFTs are unique non-fungible crypto tokens that exist on a blockchain network and cannot be replicated. You can simply convert any digital form of art into a unique NFT. Also, you can create, purchase and sell NFTS in your own NFT marketplace
How much does it cost to build an NFT?
Creating NFT will range from $10,000 to over $100,0000. The cost of developing NFTs depends on factors such as the cost of blockchain, gas fee, marketplace account fee, listing fee, etc. Ethereum and Solana are low-cost blockchain platforms you can use.
How much time will it take to build an NFT?
It may take around 5 to 8 months to create an NFT marketplace. Still, it depends on the features, functionalities, and complexity you want to incorporate into the NFT.
What are the popular blockchains for creating NFTS?
The most popular blockchains for creating non-fungible tokens are Ethereum, OpenSea, and Rarible.
