FAQs

Got specific questions? Get clear answers

What is a Smart Contract?
A smart contract is a programmed code that handles the exchange of assets or digital currencies between two parties when some pre-defined conditions are satisfied.
How long does it take for a Smart Contract development project to go live?
It all depends on the project scope and complexity that the project duration will take your time from a few weeks to several months.
What are the advantages of smart contract?
Here is a list of advantages that you can get from smart contracts.
  • Transparency
  • Cost reduction
  • Speed
  • Automatic Updates
  • Autonomy
How much does it cost to build a smart contract?
It depends on which blockchain platform you deploy the smart contracts. Mainly, smart contract are built on Ethereum, so depending on the size and complexity of smart contract, the cost varies. However, on average, the cost of developing a smart contract on the Ethereum platform would cost around $10,000-$20,000. But the exact price involves several factors like gas fee, software size, and the number of hours. Hence, the best way to get the exact estimation of smart contract development cost is to directly get a quote from the company.
Which programming language do you use in Smart contract development?
Our blockchain developers are experts in using all the programming languages in smart contract development that include Java, Rust, Vyper, Solidity, C#, C++, JavaScript, Go, and Python.
Can you make changes to my smart contract during the development phase?
Yes, we can do it easily. As we said we follow the agile methodology in our smart contract development solutions so it allows us to make changes in smart contract during the development phase. However, we advise changes at the initial stage otherwise it affects the timeline and final cost of the project.
What tools & technologies do you use for developing smart contracts
We have expert blockchain developers who hold proven experience and expertise in using popular technologies for smart contract development. And since Ethereum is the most commonly used framework for the creation of smart contracts, we have dedicated Ethereum developers.
What security standards do you follow to secure smart contract?
Our smart contract developers implement up-to-date blockchain development practices and advanced security protocols that ensure the full safety and confidentiality of transactions or data exchange in smart contract.
