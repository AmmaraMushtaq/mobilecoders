Frequently Asked Questions

What is digital product development?
Digital product development is bringing an idea into a reality by implementing the latest technologies, industry best practices, and compelling UI designs.
How much does it cost to develop a digital product?
The cost of developing a digital product depends on multiple factors, such as the development platform, technologies, complexity, location, and the number of features you want to add to your product.
What are the various phases of product development?
The following are the different phases of digital product development:
  • Product Ideation
  • Prototyping and Wireframing
  • Product Design
  • Product Development
  • Quality Assurance
  • Product Release
  • Product Maintenance and support
How long does it take to build a digital product?
The time taken to build a digital product completely depends on various factors like the development platform, the developer's experience level, the design's complexity, features and functionality, testing, and many more.
