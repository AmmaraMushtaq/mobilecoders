Features of E-Commerce Website

  • Shopping Cart

    A shopping cart is the most essential feature of an e-commerce website. It enables customers to add products to a virtual cart and purchase them in one transaction.

  • Payment Gateway Integration

    Payment gateway integration is the process of integrating a payment gateway with an e-commerce website. This allows customers to pay for their purchases securely and conveniently.

  • Responsive Design

    Responsive design ensures that the website is optimized for a range of devices, from mobile to desktop. This ensures that customers have a great experience regardless of the platform they’re using.

  • Security and Privacy

    Security and privacy are essential for any e-commerce website. SSL encryption and secure payment gateways help to protect customer data and prevent fraud.

  • Search and Filtering

    Search and filtering are important features for any e-commerce website. They allow customers to quickly find the products they’re looking for and narrow down their choices

  • Analytics and Reporting

    Analytics and reporting allow you to track customer activity and gain insights into what’s working and what’s not. This helps you make informed decisions about your website and marketing strategy.

What are all features included in the eCommerce web?
You can add as many features as your users want to make your eCommerce store more intuitive and engaging. You can include multiple features in your eCommerce web: multiple payment gateways, a quick checkout process, social login, order tracking, product sharing, and many others.
How much time does it take to make an eCommerce web?
The exact time to develop an eCommerce web depends on your project requirements. You have to create the first version of your eCommerce web final product or MVP, which will take 3 to 4 months. It will take additional months if develop a complex app or integrate advanced features.
What factors determine the eCommerce web development cost?
There are multiple factors responsible for developing a powerful and scalable mobile app. The factors that impact the eCommerce web development cost include eCommerce web design, Platform for app development, Wireframing cost, Maintenance cost, and incorporating advanced features.
