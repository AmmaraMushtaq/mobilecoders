Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build a wearable device application atMobileCoderz?
We have different pricing models depending on project size and niche. Hence, to know the exact value of your wearable app development cost, you need to share your project requirements, and then we can give you the final figure. However, to help you estimate, the average cost of developing a smartwatch app may go between $10,000-$100,000.
Do you sign an NDA to secure my app idea and data confidentiality?
We follow a strict NDA policy to secure our client's project data. We even implement essential compliances and regulations to ensure your app doesn't meet any issues after launch.
Which programming language do you use in iOS or iPhone app development?
Our developers use all programming languages for wearable and smartwatch app development. However, which programming language we use depends explicitly on the OS platform. If it is Android Wear App, then we use Kotlin and Java. And if it is an Apple Watch app, then Swift and Objective-C.
Can you make changes to my app during the app development period?
Yes, as we said, we follow the agile methodology in our wearable and smartwatch app development solutions, allowing us to accept changes in the code, UI/UX design, features, and functionalities during the development phase. However, we recommend changes at the initial stage; otherwise, it affects the timeline.
Who will control or manage my project?
We set up a dedicated team for every project that consists of project managers,developers, UI/UX designers, QAs, and business analysts. The team always keeps you in the communication channel and updates you about the progress of your project.
How much time does it take to develop a wearable app?
It completely depends on the complexity and size of the project. However, the average time to build an Android Wear and Apple watch application usually takes 4-6 months if it is designed for popular smartwatch brands or wearable devices.
Do you offer app maintenance and support after the completion of the project?
Yes, of course, we offer free 3-month app maintenance support after the completion of the project. However, depending on your project size and signing an SLA, you can get long-term support

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does IoT app development cost at your company?
IoT app development is an extremely diverse and dynamic solution. It is quite complex to give an exact figure. So, to know the cost of your IoT app development project, you need to share your requirements, and then we can provide you with a quote. However, to help you estimate, the average IoT app development cost ranges from $10,000 - $100,000.
What tools & technologies do you use for IoT app development?
Our IoT app development utilizes the most widely-used IoT SDKs and tech stacks like AWS IoT, Azure IoT, and Google Cloud IoT Core. We build all functionalities that enable our IoT apps flawlessly connect with RFID Chips, Beacons, Bluetooth Low Energy devices, sensors, trackers, and smart devices.
How much time will it take to build an IoT app?
Several factors affect the duration of IoT app development. It depends on the complexity and features of IoT applications. However, it generally takes six months to build medium-scale IoT applications. If it is enterprise-scale, then it may take up to 12 months.
