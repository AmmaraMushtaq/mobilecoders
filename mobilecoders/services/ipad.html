How much does it cost to build an iPad application at your company?
There is no certain figure since it completely depends on the features and the level of complexity of the iPad app. However, on average, iPad app development cost usually lies between $10,000-$100,000. Therefore, to know the exact value, we advise you to share your project requirements, and then we can give you the final figure.
Do you sign an NDA to secure my app idea and data confidentiality?
We follow a strict NDA policy to secure our client's project data. We even implement essential compliances and regulations to ensure your app doesn't meet any issues after launch.
Which programming language do you use in iPad app development?
Since iPads are built on the iOS platform, we use all the programming languages, such as Swift, Objective-C, and C++, for iPadOS app development. Besides this, we also use JavaScript to develop cross-platform apps for iPadOS devices.
What tools & technologies do you use for developing iPad applications?
We use Apple's native SDKs and tools that consist of Xcode, Focus Filter API, Machine Learning, ARKit 6, RoomPlan, WeatherKit, Metal 3, App Intents, and other additional resources for developing industry-specific and custom iPad applications.
