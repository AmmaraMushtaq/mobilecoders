Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build an iBeacon application at your company?
On average, iBeacon app development costs between $10,000 - $100,000. However, it is a general estimation. The exact figure depends on your project requirements and the complexity of features that your iBeacon application has. Hence, to know the exact value of your iBeacon application development cost, share your project requirements with us, and then we can give you the final figure.
What tools & technologies do you use for developing iBeacon applications?
We use Apple's native iBeacon SDK for developing apps for iBeacons. For Android, we use Android Studio. Apart from this, we also use additional tools and SDKs for developing BLE mobile apps.
Who will control or manage my iBeacon app development project?
We have a dedicated iBeacon and BLE app development team that consists of in-house developers, UI/UX designers, project managers, QAs, BLE experts, and business analysts. Our team always keeps you in the communication channel and updates you about the progress of your project.
Do you offer post-launch support after the completion of the project?
Yes, of course, we offer free 3-month app maintenance support after the completion of the project. However, you can get long-term support depending on your project size.
Can iBeacons work without a mobile app?
Yes, iBeacons can work without a mobile app. The best example is Google Eddystone beacons that transmit URLs scannable by multiple Android devices. However, the purpose of developing an app is to visualize and collect data based on user location. The app integrates with the beacons and processes the information to share on your mobile screen.
Can iBeacon apps run without Internet connectivity?
Yes, as iBeacons use Bluetooth technology, the iBeacon app can work without the internet.
Do you manufacture iBeacons yourselves?
No, we don't manufacture iBeacons. But yes, we can guide you in choosing the most suitable BLE devices for your project.
Is there any difference between iBeacon and Beacons?
Yes, iBeacons are Apple's products. iBeacons specifically work on BLE technology. In contrast, the beacon is a common name that transmits Bluetooth signals.
