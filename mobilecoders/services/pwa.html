Got specific questions? Get clear answers

How much does it cost to develop a Progressive web application at your company?
On average, developing a progressive web app costs between $4000-$10,000. However, it depends on the features and complexity of PWAs. Hence, to know the exact value of your PWA development cost, you need to share your project requirements, and then we can give you the final figure
Do you sign an NDA to secure my app idea and data confidentiality?
We follow a strict NDA policy to secure our client's project data. We even implement essential compliances and regulations to ensure your app doesn't meet any issues after launch.
Which programming language do you use in Progressive web app development?
Our developers are well-versed in using all programming languages for PWA and web app development. Here, we use PHP, C++, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, Ruby, Swift, Kotlin, and Go.
Can you make changes to my app during the development period?
Yes, we follow the agile methodology in our web app development process, allowing us to accept changes in the code, web design, features, functionalities, or anything. However, we recommend changes at the initial stage; otherwise, it affects the timeline.
What tools & technologies do you use for PWA development?
We use all the latest and most popular frameworks & technologies in our web development solutions. For developing PWAs, we use ReactJS, AngularJS, Vue.js, Magento PWA Studio, Preact, Svelte, Ionic, and Polymer.
Who will control or manage my PWA project?
We have a dedicated PWA development team comprising project managers, developers, UI/UX designers, QAs, and business analysts. The team always keeps you in the communication channel and updates you about the progress of your project.
How long does it take to create a PWA?
It generally takes three months to build a fully-functional Progressive web application. However, depending on the features, the development time may increase further.
Do you offer post-launch support after the completion of the project?
Yes, of course, we offer free 3-month app maintenance support after the completion of the project. However, you can get long-term support depending on your project size.
Which is better, an Android app or PWA for a startup?
Since PWA development costs very less than developing an Android app, the PWA app stands out as the better option for startups. PWS gives the same app-like features but on the web platform.
Can you put a progressive web app on the Google Play Store?
No, PWA can't be submitted to any app stores. PWAs are accessed via a URL link.
How Are Progressive Web Apps Different Than Regular Web Apps?
Progressive web apps are also websites. But the main difference that differentiates a PWA from regular web apps or websites is that its user interface is that of a mobile application. PWA is built on a mobile-first approach to leverage the concept of mobile apps on web platforms.
