Frequently Asked Questions

How much does your Android app development company charge for developing an Android app?
We have different pricing models depending on project size and niche. Hence, to know the exact value of your project development cost you need to share your project requirements and then we can give you the final figure. However, to help you estimate, the average cost of developing an android app may go around $10,000-$1000,000.
Do you sign an NDA to secure my app idea and data confidentiality?
Yes, we follow a very strict NDA policy to secure our client's project data. We even implement essential compliances and regulations to ensure your app doesn't meet any issues after launch.
Which programming language do you use in Android app development?
Our developers are well-versed in using all programming languages for Android app development. However, which programming language we use specifically depends on the type of app. If it is a native app then we use Java and Kotlin. And if it is cross-platform then JavaScript and Dart.
Can you make changes in my app during the app development period?
Yes, as we said we follow agile methodology in our Android app development services so it allows us to accept changes in the code, UI/UX design, features, and functionalities, or anything. However, we recommend changes at the initial stage otherwise it affects the timeline.
What tools & technologies do you use for Android app development?
We use all the latest and essential technologies in our Android application development. We have full-stack development resources for building all types of apps including custom Android app development.
Who will control or manage my Android app development project?
We create a dedicated team for every project that consists of project managers, developers, UI/UX designers, QAs, and business analysts. The team always keeps you in the communication channel and updates you about the progress of your project.
Do you offer post-launch support after the completion of the project?
Yes, of course, we offer free 3-month app maintenance support after the completion of the project. However, you can get long-term support depending on your project size.
