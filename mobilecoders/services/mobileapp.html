Got Specific Questions? Get Clear Answers

How much does it cost to build a mobile app at MobileCoderz?
It is extremely difficult to estimate an exact cost since it depends on various factors. However, on average, the cost of developing a mobile app generally goes around $10,000 - $100,000 for small or medium size projects. Here, we have different pricing models. Hence, we advise you to share your project requirements with us, and then we can give you an exact cost evaluation of your app project.
Which programming languages do you use in your mobile app development process?
We use all programming languages. Our developers are proficient in using every programming language for native and cross-platform development for both platforms, Android and iOS.
Can you make changes to my app in the middle of the app development stage?
Yes. Since we are a professional mobile app development company so we follow the agile methodology in our app development process. This enables us to make changes to the code, UI/UX design, features, and functionalities in the middle of the development period. However, we advise changes at the initial stage; otherwise, it affects the project completion timeline and cost.
What tools & technologies do you use for mobile app development?
We use all the latest and widely-used tools & technologies for mobile app development. We have a big team of different professionals who dedicatedly hold qualified skills in all areas, including front-end, back-end, UI/UX design, QA & testing, cloud, and API integration.
Who will control or manage my project?
We set up a dedicated team for every project that consists of project managers, developers, UI/UX designers, QAs, and business analysts. The team always keeps you in the communication channel and updates you about the progress of your project. Since you are the owner of the project, we assign full rights of the project to you only with mutual consent.
Do you offer app maintenance and support after the completion of the project?
Yes, of course, we offer free 3-month app maintenance support after the completion of the project. However, depending on your project size and signing an SLA, you can get long-term support.
How much time does it take to build a mobile app?
Generally, it takes 4-6 months to build a fully-functional mobile application. However, the timeline of the complete development depends on the project size. If it is a small-scale app and needs popular features, it can be delivered within 2-3 months. But, if it is enterprise-scale and requires complex and advanced features, it may also take around 6-12 months.
